Help Scout IA

Overview Help Scout spun up a working group that would explore the future of AI, and I joined as its Product Designer. The goal was to explore the potential of AI in the context of Help Scout and to determine if it was worth investing in. We were tasked with iterating quickly and learning as much as possible. I leaned into spinning up prototypeable ideas and quickly testing them with the team.

Help Scout Articles

Overview Help Scout Docs is a knowledge base tool originally designed for small businesses. In 2021, after a period of dormancy, the product was revived with a newly dedicated team. The goal was to make Docs a product that could scale to large enterprises, while still being easy to use for small businesses. I joined as the teams product designer, focusing on revamping the article composer experience.


Figma Plugins

Getting creative When Help Scout moved from Sketch to Figma it opened up a new creative avenue for me to explore in the shape of Figma Plugins; a way to extended the functionality of Figma by creating custom programs that can be used within the app. Since the switch I've designed and coded several. Some are internal, centered around Help Scouts design system, and some are published to the Figma community, in use by thousands of designers worldwide. Play around with the interactive prototypes below to get a sense for how the plugins work.
