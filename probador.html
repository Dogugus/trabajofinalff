Figma Plugins

Getting creative When Help Scout moved from Sketch to Figma it opened up a new creative avenue for me to explore in the shape of Figma Plugins; a way to extended the functionality of Figma by creating custom programs that can be used within the app. Since the switch I've designed and coded several. Some are internal, centered around Help Scouts design system, and some are published to the Figma community, in use by thousands of designers worldwide.
